# Getting started with Cadence

Cadence turns your website URL into a marketing workspace: strategy, SEO audits, and ready-to-edit content.

## Before you begin

You need:

1. Your website URL (e.g. `https://yoursite.com`)
2. A Cadence account (sign in at your Cadence URL → **Get started**)
3. For **direct WordPress publish**: cloud sign-in + WordPress connected in **Settings → Integrations**

## The 3-step workflow

### Step 1 — Run your first audit

1. Sign in and paste your site URL.
2. Choose your growth goal and brand voice (or let Cadence infer them).
3. Wait for the analysis to finish — you land on the **Dashboard**.

The dashboard shows your brand summary, readiness score, strengths/gaps, and a **Create** section.

### Step 2 — Fix SEO (optional but recommended)

1. Click **SEO Agent** in the top nav.
2. Enter your site URL and run a **Quick crawl** (free) or **Deep crawl** (Pro).
3. Review tabs:
   - **Overview** — scores and summary
   - **Technical crawl** — broken links, speed issues
   - **Meta tags** — suggested titles & descriptions
   - **Content plan** — topic ideas (not full articles)

Use this to decide *what* to write before you open the content studio.

### Step 3 — Create marketing content

On the **Dashboard**, scroll to **Create your first marketing piece** and pick a type:

| Type | What you get |
|------|----------------|
| **Keyword list** | Search terms to target |
| **Blog post** | Full article + WordPress export |
| **Social posts** | X, LinkedIn, Facebook drafts |
| **Email series** | 3-email welcome sequence |
| **Lead magnet** | Free download offer copy |

Click **Generate** → Cadence opens **Campaign Studio** with your draft.

## After you generate something

In Campaign Studio you can:

- **Edit** the text directly
- **Refine** with AI (“make it shorter”, “more SEO”, etc.)
- **Generate image** (Imagen) for blog heroes
- **Copy WordPress HTML** or **Publish** (see [How to post](./HOW_TO_POST.md))
- **Download** or **Export** campaign ZIP from Campaign History

## Saving your work

- **Cloud (signed in):** Work auto-saves every few seconds.
- **Brand switcher (top right):** Each client/site has its own saved workspace.
- **Campaign History:** Saved audit runs and versions — separate from the brand list.

## New audit vs new brand site

| Action | Button | What it does |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **New audit** | Top nav `+ New audit` | Re-analyze a URL; saves to Campaign History |
| **Add brand site** | Brand dropdown → Add brand site | New client workspace (agency / multi-site) |
